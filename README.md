### 3+ scenes
There are three scenes (not including the start), which are the logo, menu screen, and loading screen.

### 2+ audio assets
In the audio folder, there is 'fire.mp3', which is used in the loading screen to make a crackling fire noise,  and 'thud-sfx.mp3', which is used in the logo scene to make the sound of the peach hitting the ground.

### 2+ image assets
In the assets folder, there are four png files. One is for a peach, one is the title, one is a firepit, and one is a cloud of smoke. The peach gets used in the logo scene and the title scene, the title gets used in the title scene, the firepit gets used in the loading scene, and the cloud of smoke is used in the loading scene.

### 2+ geometric shapes
There are two geometric shapes used. Both are rectangles. The first appears in the menu screen to create a fade to black transition by going from transparent to fully opaque. The second appears in the loading screen as the background to make it black.

### 1+ logotype text
The title png mentioned earlier is used as the title/logo for the game's menu screen.

### 1+ multiline text block
The menu screen has a large block of text that says Start, settings and credits over 6 lines. There are gaps for spacing reasons and also to create space for the continue block of text, which is greyed out.

### 1+ fading scene transition
As mentioned earlier, the black rectangle becomes opaque to fade the menu scene to black.

### 1+ object motion animation
There are several, although the most noteworthy are probably the peach hitting the ground and rolling during the logo scene and the smoke continuously rising out of the firepit. The peach falls to the ground (as if it was thrown) and rolls a bit before coming to a stop in the logo scene and the smoke continuously appears, rises, and disappears during the loading scene.
